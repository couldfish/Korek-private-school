About Our School

Korek Private Primary School, located in the heart of Soran, Erbil, is a growing educational institution committed to academic excellence and strong moral values. With approximately 700–800 students, we provide a supportive and inspiring learning environment.

Our mission is to provide a safe, innovative, and student-centered learning environment that empowers learners to reach their full potential. We aim to become one of the leading educational institutions in the Kurdistan Region.
